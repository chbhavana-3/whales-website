THE STORIES

Home > The Stories

Whales Animation was founded by Santhosh Beeram, a visionary who deeply believes in the principles of quality, analysis, and community service. Santhosh’s commitment to excellence was not just about producing top‑notch visual effects but also about nurturing a culture of knowledge‑sharing among fellow Whalers. He always had his eyes on the future, valuing long‑term growth over short‑term gains.

From this solid foundation, Whales Animations was born—a natural extension of Whales Animation. With the same dedication to quality and a forward‑looking approach, Whales Animations carries on the legacy, pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation in the animation industry.
