PRE-PRODUCTION
Matte Paint Concept Art Animatics Story Board Modeling Texturing Rigging

At Whales Animation, we specialize in bringing your creative vision to life through our comprehensive pre-production services. Our skilled team excels in:

Matte Painting: Crafting stunning backgrounds that enhance your project’s visual depth.
Concept Art: Developing unique designs that set the tone and style for your production.
Animatics: Creating preliminary animations that visualize the flow of your story.
Storyboarding: Mapping out scenes to ensure a seamless narrative progression.
Modeling: Building detailed 3D models that breathe life into your characters and environments.
Texturing: Applying realistic textures to enhance visual fidelity.
Rigging: Preparing models for animation, ensuring smooth movement and flexibility.
